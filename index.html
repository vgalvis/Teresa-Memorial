In Loving Memory Of

Teresa Ailene DeArmond

1957-2020

Teresa Ailene was born on September 2, 1957, in Medford, Oregon. She was baptized on November 25, 1972, and married four years later on September 4 of 1976. She is survived by her dear husband Jerry, her son Brandon, daughter-in-law Leah, her loving mother Pat, and her older brothers John and Joe Netherland, four stepsisters and numerous nieces and nephews.

Despite having endured meningitis at age 6, a severe car accident when she was 11 years old, resulting in the loss of her dear father Jess, and a long battle with rheumatoid arthritis starting at just 18, she has happily pioneered for over 14 years. She is known for her kindness, generosity, infectious smile, and laugh. Whether it was working with RBC and LDC, assisting others in the congregation, in the ministry, at the meetings, on vacation, or just visiting friends, Teresa always put the needs of others ahead of her own. We long for the day when we can hear her laugh again, and welcome her back with open arms.
